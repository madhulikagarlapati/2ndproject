JAGUAR is a British luxury car manufacturer based in Coventry, England. The company is known for its elegant, high-performance vehicles and has a rich history in motorsports. Jaguar's cars are characterized by their distinctive design, powerful engines, and luxurious interiors. The brand is synonymous with sophistication and performance. Jaguar's lineup includes a range of vehicles, from sedans to SUVs and high-performance sports cars.
