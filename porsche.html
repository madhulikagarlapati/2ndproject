PORSHE is a German automobile manufacturer that designs, engineers, produces, markets and distributes luxury vehicles. Porsche is a member of the Volkswagen Group and has its roots at Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. Porsche-branded vehicles are produced in nine production facilities worldwide. Porsche is one of the best-selling luxury automotive brands in the world, delivering 1.8 million vehicles globally in 2020. The company is known for its slogan "Vorsprung durch Technik" ("Advancement through Technology") and has a reputation for producing high-quality, technologically advanced vehicles with a focus on performance and design. Porsche's lineup includes a range of vehicles, from compact cars to SUVs and high-performance sports
